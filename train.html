2. Vocational & Skill-Based Training for Youth:

We actively promote vocational and technical training to encourage self-reliance and employability among youth and women, especially in underserved regions. Our programs include:

  • Information Technology (IT) Fundamentals
  • Freelancing & Remote Work Skills
  • Web and Graphic Design
  • Handicrafts & Sewing Courses (for both males and females)

These skill development initiatives aim to foster financial independence and contribute to community development by creating pathways to sustainable employment and entrepreneurship.
